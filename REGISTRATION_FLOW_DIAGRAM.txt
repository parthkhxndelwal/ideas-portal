================================================================================
                        FRESHER-ONLY REGISTRATION
================================================================================

Alternative endpoint for Mr. & Mrs. Fresher competition
File: /api/v1/fresher-registration

Separate flow:
1. Submit Roll Number
   └─ Validate student exists
   └─ Check batch year (2025 batch only for 2026 event)
   └─ Send OTP to krmu email

2. Verify OTP
   └─ Verify 6-digit OTP

3. Confirm Registration
   └─ Create registration
   └─ isKrmu: true
   └─ isFresher: true
   └─ feePaid: true (FREE for fresher competition)
   └─ QR code auto-generated
   └─ Email sent

Result: Instant ticket, no payment required
